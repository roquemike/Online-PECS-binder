Hello and Welcome to the Online PECS Binder!

A PECS Binder using an "I want" picture

PECS stands for Picture Exchange Communication System.
The PECS helps anyone with communication challenges, such as persons with Autism, communicate through the exchange of pictures.
The pictures which make up the vocublary of the individual are collected in a binder and the pictures are "exchanged"

PECS have different phases and The Online Binder is designed for use in the 4th Phase,
where we are able to use an “I want” picture followed by a picture of the item being requested.
Learn more about PECS from Pyramid Educational Consultants who developed the PECS -
https://pecs.com/picture-exchange-communication-system-pecs/
